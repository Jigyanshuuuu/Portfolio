About

Curiosity, mostly.

I'm Jigyanshu, a second-year B.Sc. Mathematics student at the University of Delhi with a passion for building things that combine logic, creativity, and curiosity.

My interests span pure mathematics, software development, machine learning, filmmaking, video editing, graphic design, writing, and entrepreneurship. I enjoy exploring ideas across disciplines and turning them into meaningful projects — whether that's developing software, designing interfaces, editing cinematic videos, or writing stories.

I'm currently learning full-stack development, artificial intelligence, UI/UX design, and advanced mathematics, while documenting my creative journey as a beginner video editor and designer. My long-term goal is to pursue graduate research in pure mathematics at a leading university, while building products, creative work, and businesses that leave a lasting impact.
